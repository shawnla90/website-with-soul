# Blog Post Template

> Copy this template when creating a new blog post. Fill in the frontmatter, replace the skeleton sections with your content, and delete this instruction block.

---

## Frontmatter

```markdown
---
title: "[FILL IN: Post title. Under 60 characters for SEO. Be specific.]"
date: "[FILL IN: YYYY-MM-DD]"
description: "[FILL IN: Meta description. Under 155 characters. Summarize the value, not the topic.]"
tags: ["[FILL IN]", "[FILL IN]"]
---
```

### Field Guide

| Field | Purpose | Tips |
|---|---|---|
| `title` | Displayed on the page and in search results | Keep under 60 characters. Front-load the keyword. |
| `date` | Publication date. Used for sorting. | Format: YYYY-MM-DD |
| `description` | Meta description for search engines and social cards | Under 155 characters. Describe what the reader gets, not what you wrote. |
| `tags` | Categorization. Used for filtering and related posts. | 2-4 tags. Be consistent with existing tags. |

## Quality Checklist

Before publishing, verify:

- [ ] Title is under 60 characters and contains the target keyword
- [ ] Description is under 155 characters and describes reader value
- [ ] Opening paragraph hooks the reader within 2 sentences
- [ ] Every section has at least one specific detail (code, number, example)
- [ ] No filler paragraphs (every paragraph earns its place)
- [ ] Ran through anti-slop rules (0-2 flags max)
- [ ] Code snippets are tested and accurate
- [ ] All links work
- [ ] Read it on the local dev server, not just in the editor
- [ ] Date in frontmatter matches actual publish date
