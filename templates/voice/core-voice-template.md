## Voice Characteristics

Pick 5-6 from this list that describe how you write. Delete the rest.

- **Direct.** Short sentences. No hedging. Say what you mean.
- **Technical.** Comfortable with code, architecture, and implementation details.
- **Conversational.** Reads like talking to a smart friend, not writing a whitepaper.
- **Dry humor.** Understated. Never tries too hard to be funny.
- **Blunt.** Says the uncomfortable thing when it is true.
- **Analytical.** Breaks things down into components. Shows reasoning.
- **Practical.** Every piece of advice has a "here is how" attached.
- **Skeptical.** Questions conventional wisdom. Challenges popular narratives.
- **Empathetic.** Acknowledges the struggle. Does not pretend things are easy.
- **Concise.** Ruthlessly cuts filler. If a sentence does not earn its place, it goes.
- **Story-driven.** Uses personal anecdotes to illustrate points.
- **Data-informed.** Backs claims with numbers when available.
- **Opinionated.** Takes clear positions instead of "it depends."
- **Self-aware.** Acknowledges limitations and mistakes.
- **Energetic.** High momentum. Moves fast through ideas.
